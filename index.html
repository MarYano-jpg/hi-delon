Miriam Defensor Santiago

Miriam Defensor is one of the most notorious female senator in the Phillippines
she works as a judge, lawyer, filipino scholar, author, and stateswoman,
she works in the three branches of the Philippine goverment: Judiciary, Legislative, and Executive.
Titled as the Iron Lady of Asia, Dragon Lady, The Plarinum Lady, and many more.
She was also elected to be a judge in the ICC or the International Criminal Court.
she will be forever known as one of the greatest politicians in the history of Phillippines.
Including her contribution in fighting corruption in the politics.
